Mochi Kimchi: Delivering best J-Style Kimchi to Healthier You!

甘っ辛っうまっ‼な味わい | Sweet, Spicy, and Delicious! Kimchi

The savory flavor of bonito flakes and other seafood is perfectly blended with a rich, ingredient-packed seasoning, making it irresistibly good with rice—so much so that you'll find yourself wanting seconds. Compared to regular kimchi, it is milder in spiciness, making it enjoyable even for children who may not like spicy foods. Not only is it delicious on its own, but it is also highly praised as a versatile ingredient for cooking. This kimchi is enhanced with our unique plant-based lactic acid bacteria, Pne-12 (Pine-12).
